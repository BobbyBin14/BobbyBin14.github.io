Concept
The first procedure of the project.

Students are requested to design a four-limbed humanoid character, excluding robots, with an associated biography. The concept should encapsulate the "Vietnamese antihero" theme. This challenges to weave cultural narratives with modern design.

Software using: Photoshop
02
Model & Texture
The second procedure of the project.

Evolving from the initial design, this phase meticulously crafts the character's 3D presence, blending modeling, UV mapping, and refined texturing.Workflow: In Blender, sculpt the character faithfully to the concept and tailor its attire. Execute precise UV unwrapping. Progress to Substance Painter, enhancing with detailed textures, priming the character for its spotlight moment.

Software using: Blender & Substance Painter

Rig & Animation
The third procedure of the project.

This procedure emphasizes skeletal articulation, weight distribution, and motion infusion. Initiate by constructing a coherent rig tailored to the character's anatomy in Blender. Precision in weight painting ensures lifelike movements and deformations. Conclude by animating a foundational walk cycle, bestowing the character with its inaugural stride. Every step is diligently orchestrated in Blender, ensuring fluidity and realism in motion.

Software using: Blender

VJ Performance
3D Model & Animation

In a collaborative venture with my talented peers, our group embarked on an immersive VJ project, meticulously choreographed to a chosen techno track from the Free Music Archive. Our mission was to synthesize auditory and visual artistry, ensuring a seamless blend and a captivating experience. Our strategic approach entailed: Conceptualization: Devising a cohesive visual theme that resonates with the techno vibes. This step was pivotal, setting the stage for all subsequent visual productions. Division of Labor: Establishing clear roles and responsibilities, we made certain every member had a distinct yet harmonious part in the creation process. Asset Production: Our suite of visuals boasted: Dynamic motion graphics via After Effects. 3D animations sculpted in Blender, a realm where I led the charge, translating our vision into fluid movements and engaging designs. Effects crafted in Resolume, adding layers of depth and intrigue to our live VJ session. Lastly, the entire ensemble came together in class. Our presentation delineated the narrative behind our visuals, followed by a live VJ performance, where our creations danced in symphony with the techno beats. Through this endeavor, my prowess in 3D design and animation played a pivotal role, showcasing my technical expertise and artistic flair.

Software using: Blender
